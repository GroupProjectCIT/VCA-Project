About Us:

Virtual Care Assistant (VCA)
With advances in modern medicine, less people in the developed world are dying from the traditional killers of heart disease and cancer. As a consequence, people are now living longer. However the benefits of old age has brought new challenges and we have witnessed an explosion in the incidence of Alzheimer's and dementia. In America alone, it is estimated that 5.5 million people are currently living with the disease.

We provide a traditional service of providing human care assistants to the elderly. Our website acts as a control centre for companies involved in the provision of in-home care.
On our app, it is more than a tool, but is more like a friend. It is always there for the user. It can help you stay focused with activities like memory skills, calendar playback, location aware and much more.
